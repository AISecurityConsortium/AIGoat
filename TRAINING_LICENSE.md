# Training Content License

AI Goat's labs, prompts, challenge walkthroughs, and workshop materials are
designed to help people learn about AI security. We want this content to stay
free and accessible for learners and researchers.

## What's covered

The following directories contain training content:

- `app/challenges/` — dynamic flag generation engine and exploit evaluators (attack pattern detection, success thresholds, multi-turn analysis logic)
- `prompts/` — system prompts for defense levels, labs, and challenges
- `docs/` — workshop guides, challenge walkthroughs, and documentation
- `media/` — images, logos, and visual training assets
- `config/labs.yml` — attack lab definitions and scenario descriptions

## What you can do freely

- Use AI Goat for personal learning and self-study
- Run the labs and challenges on your own machine
- Use it in academic courses at schools and universities (non-commercial)
- Modify the content for your own research
- Share modified versions under the same license
- Write blog posts, articles, or talks that reference AI Goat content

## What requires permission

If you want to use AI Goat training content in a **commercial** setting, you
need prior written permission from the maintainers. This includes:

- **Paid training courses** that use AI Goat labs or prompts
- **Commercial workshops** where attendees pay to participate
- **Certification programs** built from AI Goat challenges or walkthroughs
- **Corporate training programs** delivered for a fee
- **Reselling or repackaging** AI Goat training materials
